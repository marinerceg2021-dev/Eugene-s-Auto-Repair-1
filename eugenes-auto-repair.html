What We Do

Expert Auto Services

From routine maintenance to complex electrical repairs — we handle it all with the expertise and integrity you deserve.

General Auto Repair

Brakes, suspension, steering, exhaust, cooling systems, and all routine mechanical repairs. We fix what others misdiagnose.

Diagnostics

Accurate computer diagnostics and thorough inspection. We find the real problem — not the most expensive one. Dealers have charged customers $2,500 for what Eugene fixed for a fraction of the cost.

Electrical & Alternator

Wiring gremlins, alternator failures, starter issues, battery replacement and charging system diagnostics. Eugene is known for being "fantastic with wiring gremlins."

E-Check (Free)

Ohio emissions testing at no charge. Fast, professional, and we pull your car right away so you can be on your way in minutes.

Preventive Maintenance

Oil changes, tire rotations, fluid flushes, filter replacements, and scheduled tune-ups to keep your vehicle running strong and saving you money long-term.

Tow & Emergency

Stranded and need help fast? Eugene's team will guide you through your tow and get your vehicle diagnosed and repaired — even on short notice or near closing time.
